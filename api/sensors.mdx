Energy Control Pro provides 9 sensor entities that monitor your solar power system's state, power flows, and energy management actions.

## Power Monitoring Sensors

### Solar Power

<ParamField path="sensor.energy_control_pro_solar_power" type="sensor">

  <ResponseField name="key" type="string" default="solar_w">

  </ResponseField>

  <ResponseField name="device_class" type="string" default="power">

  </ResponseField>

</ParamField>

## Entity ID Format

All sensor entity IDs follow the pattern: `sensor.energy_control_pro_{sensor_name}`

Example entity IDs:
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_solar_power`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_load_power`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_surplus_power`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_grid_import_power`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_grid_export_power`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_energy_state`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_export_duration`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_import_duration`
- `sensor.energy_control_pro_last_action`
